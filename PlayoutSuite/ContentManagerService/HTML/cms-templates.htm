Content Manager Service (CMS)

Templates

When you want to add similar elements to several slides, you can create a template and just call it on each slide.

The templates are defined in templates files.

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?> 
<templates> 
    <template id="%templateID%"> 
        <!-- elements--> 
    </template>
    <!-- other templates... -->
</templates>

To call a template, just add this element:

<template id="%templateID%"/>
<template id="header-littlelogo"/>
